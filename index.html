Operating since 1993, Air Ghana has become established as a premier Ghanaian registered cargo & passenger airline, GSSA and Air Logistics company providing, bespoke services to the European, Middle East & African regions alongside airport representation, aircraft handling and support, cargo general sales & services and all aspects of aviation ground & cargo handling services.

To meet the growing demands of the oil, gas and mining industries and ensure its capability to provide for the movement of outsized & heavy specialist cargo, Air Ghana together with West Wing Aviation further invested in the purchase of theirown Trepel Champ 350 high loader. The Trepel Champ 350 offers the unique capability of handling exceptional loads weighing up to 35 metric tons arriving and departing Accra, Kotoka International Airport
